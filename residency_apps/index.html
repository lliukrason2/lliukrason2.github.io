Residency Specialties: Application Patterns By Race

According to the American Association of Medical Colleges, each year around 20,000 M.Ds graduate and enter residency. Where do they go? Well, one can't go somewhere that one didn't apply. According to my analysis, race may play a factor in what specialties candidates choose to apply to.

By Lily Liu-Krason

Published August 2019
